Each experience responds to what students know, identifies what still needs attention, and directs them through focused repair and bridge practice before they move forward.

How it works

More than a question bank

Each quest uses performance, question history, and learning objectives to shape what students encounter next.

01

Practice

Students retrieve concepts through structured questions, calculations, applications, and challenges.

02

Repair

A missed question can trigger focused support aimed at the specific misconception or skill gap.

03

Bridge

Follow-up questions help students reconnect repaired knowledge to the original learning objective.

04

Master

Students finish with a diagnostic report showing accuracy, strengths, weaknesses, timing, and recommended next steps.
